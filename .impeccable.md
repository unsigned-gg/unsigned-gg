# unsigned — Design Canon

This repo (`unsigned-gg/unsigned-gg`, served at unsigned.gg) is the **canonical
home of the unsigned brand and design system**. Other surfaces (cluster-control-panel,
internal PWAs) derive from here.

## Design Context

### Users
New platform engineers onboarding to the unsigned platform (primary — /learn is
their front door: prove access, then understand every subsystem). They read
spec sheets, not marketing. Their job: go from zero to "I can operate this
platform" — and feel **competent and confident** doing it. They are
terminal-native people; respect that.

There is no prospect audience. unsigned is **not and will never be a public
platform** (operator directive 2026-07-10): no customers, no GA, no self-serve
signup. It is the platform, the jokes, the dev and operator playground, and
the mothership the rest of the estate operates from. Never justify a design or
copy decision by conversion, acquisition, or reach.

The outside reader the landing serves is a **founder doing diligence**. The
page is a dataroom, not a funnel: real stack, real architecture, real costs —
presented so the founder feels respected. Transparency about pricing is
identity, not sales: cerebral is not a platform-services company; it
internalizes first-rate platform services the way YC internalizes branding.
Anything published still passes Data honesty below.

### Brand Personality
**Hacker, confident, playful.** The name is C-type wordplay (`unsigned` — no
sign bit, zero-indexed, `0x` hex, overflow, `0xFFFFFFFF` max-capacity) and the
identity leans into it — but as wit, not cosplay. Easter eggs are welcome and
should be *discovered*, never shouted. Tone of voice: terse, precise, a little
dry. Say less; mean it.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark instrument panel. Near-black `#0a0a0a` ground, signal green `#00e599` as
THE accent (blue/purple/orange/red exist as semantic colors only), faint 60px
grid overlay, `JetBrains Mono` for labels/data/code, `Inter` for prose.
References: **Stripe-docs interactivity** (inline widgets that teach by doing),
**Linear restraint** (disciplined spacing, quiet color, keyboard-first).
Anti-references (hard NOs): SaaS-template card grids, gradient text,
glassmorphism, hero-metric layouts, cyberpunk neon glow, matrix rain,
terminal cosplay, forms without a backend (the old waitlist form lied — never
again), dead links (nav items must resolve).

### Tokens — SOURCE OF TRUTH
`packages/tokens/tokens.json` is canonical (`@unsigned-gg/tokens`, OPS-496);
`build.mjs` generates `dist/tokens.{css,js}`, checked in and CI-gated against
the source. Every surface consumes it: landing + `/learn` + `404` link
`/tokens.css` from the site root, `apps/onboard` imports the workspace
package, and the claude.ai `unsigned` design project mirrors `tokens.css` via
`.design-sync.json`. Change `tokens.json` → `bun run build` → propagate
everywhere. Never re-declare token values in a surface.
Core: ink `#0a0a0a` · brand green `#00e599` · blue `#4d9fff` · purple
`#a78bfa` · Inter (sans) + JetBrains Mono · 60px engineering grid backdrop.

### Design Principles
1. **Teach by doing.** Prefer an interactive widget over a paragraph. Every
   explainer has something to click, step through, or toggle.
2. **Motion conveys state.** Animate entrances/exits/data changes only;
   exponential ease-out; nothing decorative; `prefers-reduced-motion` honored
   everywhere.
3. **Keyboard-first.** Everything reachable and operable by keyboard; a
   spotlight command bar (Cmd/Ctrl-K) is the primary navigation on every page;
   visible, designed focus states.
4. **The bit is the brand.** Deploy the C-type motifs (hex addresses, bit
   fields, `0x` prefixes, the missing sign bit) sparingly and precisely — one
   good bit joke per view beats ten.
5. **Restraint outside, wit inside.** Structure is austere; personality lives
   in microcopy, empty states, and easter eggs.
6. **AA, always.** WCAG AA contrast on all text, full keyboard operability,
   designed focus rings. Ship nothing that fails this.

### Conventions
- Landing (`index.html`) and `/learn` are static, no build step — keep them
  that way. Built apps (e.g. the onboard frontend, OPS-394) live in their own
  subdirectories with build tooling scoped there.
- Squared corners; 1px `rgba(255,255,255,0.06)` borders; green reserved for
  brand accents and success, never body text.

### Data honesty (public site)
The site is public (Cloudflare Pages). Never embed secrets or tailnet/internal
hostnames, IPs, or usernames. Live-looking data must be either (a) sanitized
snapshots published by CI, or (b) clearly-labeled demo data — never a silent
fake, never a leaked real.
